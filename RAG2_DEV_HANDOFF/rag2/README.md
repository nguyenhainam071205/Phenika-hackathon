## Muc tieu

RAG2 nhan `Doctor-Revised JSON` sau khi bac si chinh sua tren viewer, sau do:

- Tong hop findings thanh bao cao nhat quan
- Ap dung ngon ngu bao cao theo chuan BYT va thuat ngu ky thuat tham chieu
- Sinh bao cao song ngu Viet/Anh
- Tra ve output JSON phuc vu buoc duyet va xuat bao cao
